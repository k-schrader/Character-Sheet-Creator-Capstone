Dragonborn

Covered in scales, claws sprouting from their hands, and the pride of the dragons within their souls. Either born from a dragon parent or blessed by a Draconic God, being half dragon has its perks.

Dwarf

Short, bold, proud, and courageous! Dwarves are excellent warriors and even better craftsmen. They live up to 400 years old and can hold a grudge the whole time.

Elf

Beautiful, graceful, and magical. Elves love nature and magic and are thought to be descended from fairies. They live long lives (more than 700 years!) and they fill their time meditating in the tall trees of ancient forests

Orc

Strong, ambitious, and proud. The very first Orcs were created by the God of war and fury! Some Orcs embrace this history and LOVE to fight, while others reject this history and prefer to use their strength in different ways. Orcs feel their emotions very strongly. They laugh loudly when happy, cry hard when sad, and sometimes they rage when they’re mad

Halfling

Cheerful, peace-loving, and loyal. Halfings are very small, standing only 3 feet tall! Friends and family are very important, and a Halfling will go to great lengths to protect those important to them. They are generous and will share anything they have. They love to discover new things

Human

Adaptable, ambitious, and adventure-seeking. If you’re reading this, you probably know what a human is! They strive to achieve great things in their lives and long to make fantastic discoveries. Humans are innovative and smart and get along well with all other races

Tiefling

Self-reliant, suspicious and cursed. Tieflings are born half demon, with large horns, red skin, and blackened eyes. This however does not make them evil! Some tieflings work very hard to be good and surprise the people who doubt them. Other tieflings submit to their origins and let the evil take over.

Barbarian

Flex your muscles and RAGE! Barbarians know how to take a hit, and they know how to deal them even better.

Bard

Strum your lute and charm your enemies! Bards support their team with a magical song and a charming smile.

Cleric

Granted healing powers by the Gods themselves, a Cleric is a blessed magical addition to your team.

Druid

One with nature and friend to all animals! A Druid channels magic of plants and animals.

Fighter

Brave knights take up swords and shields and leap into batttle! Masters of weapons and of battles.

Paladin

A holy protector! Blessed with both magic from the Gods and great strength in battle, a paladin is the best of both worlds.

Rogue

Slip into shadow and disappear. Rogues pick locks just as well as they pick pockets.

Sorcerer

Born with magic in their blood, a Sorcerer is a very powerful mage since childhood.

Warlock

Locked in a deal with a dark God, Warlocks are granted magic power from somewhere beyond.

Wizard

Filled with a desire to gain magical knowledge, Wizrads have learned the secrets to magic all by themselves.
